The Best Way to Make Sunny Side Up Eggs!

Here, you'll find very specific and delicate instructions on how to make your sunny sideup eggs the best that they can be.

What You'll need to make the most fabulous sunny side up eggs (ever):
3 jumbo eggs
1 pan with a lid or an additional pan
1 tsp margarine
a splash of water (with sound effects)

Preparing The Eggs!

1. Crack your knuckles and put on some good music
2. Slap the margerine into the pan, and turn the stove up to high to melt the it.
3. Crack the eggs into the pan, naming each one as you do so.
4. When the eggs have began to keep their shape, turn the stove down to medium/low and spash the water on them, getting water everywhere.
5. Cover the eggs and let them cook for a few minutes, until they tell you that they're ready.
6. Delicatley slide the everso heaveley egg meal that you've just created onto a plate.

Eating the eggs

1. Eat the eggs.
